PRIVACY POLICY

VSTech Limited is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our websites, mobile applications, and other online products and services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the Services.

COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

We collect information about you in a variety of ways when you use the Services.

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you create an account, sign up for newsletters, respond to a survey, or communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Services, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of the Services. This information may include your IP address, device type, operating system version, and the dates and times that you access the Services. We may also collect location data, including location data from your device or IP address.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

We and our service providers may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about you when you use the Services. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device or computer and allow us to recognize you and your preferences when you return to the Services. We may also use tracking technologies to collect information about your use of the Services and to customize and improve the Services. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on the Services.

  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities.
